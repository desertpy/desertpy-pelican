Meetups are no longer synced to this site

Sun 15 September 2019
By Nelson Chen

In posts.

tags: meetup

Unfortunately, Meetup.com has made it extremely hard to pull meetup data from their API in an automated way by a server.

We are no longer able to sync meetup posts to this site. Please check out the meetup.com group in the menu bar of this site for the latest meetup information.
